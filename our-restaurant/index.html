About Bumble & Thyme

Bumble & Thyme’s Southern cuisine is enhanced by our commitment to partnering with local farmers. At Bumble & Thyme, you can rest assured that our chefs will only be preparing your meals with locally-sourced ingredients that are organic.

The Place

Slightly north of Asheville, serenely tucked away in the foothills of the Great Smoky Mountains, you’ll find Bumble & Thyme, a 20-acre organic farm where you’ll find our restaurant and marketplace.
